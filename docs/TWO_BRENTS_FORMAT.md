# Two Brents Default Video Format

## Decision

For V1, the default editorial format is a conversation between:

- Brent Before AI
- Brent After AI

The system should not choose among multiple presenter formats by default. It should first attempt to express the lesson through the Two Brents conversation.

Solo-presenter videos are exceptions and require an explicit editorial reason.

## Purpose

The conversation is a teaching device. It lets the audience hear both:

- the earned judgment, lived experience, and practical skepticism of Brent Before AI
- the amplified capability, experimentation, and new leverage of Brent After AI

Neither Brent is superior. Neither character mocks, lectures, or looks down on the other.

## Conversation Law

Each exchange must advance at least one of these:

1. clarify the viewer's real question
2. expose a mistaken assumption
3. connect an old principle to a new AI capability
4. introduce a useful example, story, demonstration, or application
5. sharpen the practical next action

Do not add dialogue merely to remind the viewer that two characters exist.

## Default Dynamic

Brent Before AI may:

- raise the experienced seller's objection
- provide historical context
- question whether a tool is useful or merely novel
- supply a lived story or relationship lesson
- identify what technology cannot replace

Brent After AI may:

- translate technical concepts
- demonstrate new leverage
- connect separate ideas quickly
- propose an experiment or workflow
- identify what has become newly possible

Either Brent may be correct. Experience sometimes wins. New leverage sometimes wins. The useful answer may require both.

## Visual Identification

Brent Before AI defaults to:

- warmer, natural lighting
- familiar styling
- measured delivery
- no artificial eye effect

Brent After AI defaults to:

- sharper or slightly cooler lighting
- slightly faster, more connected delivery
- a brief restrained eye glint, catchlight, or activation cue
- an optional short audio cue

The activation effect must remain brief and cinematic, not cartoonish or superhero-like.

## Solo Exception Gate

A solo format is allowed only when one of these is true:

- the material is an uninterrupted personal story
- the video is a direct demonstration where dialogue would obstruct clarity
- the segment is a short announcement or correction
- Brent explicitly approves the solo treatment

The script or outline must state the reason for the exception.

## V1 Evaluation

The Two Brents conversation is the default hypothesis, not an untouchable permanent rule.

After real drafts and produced videos exist, evaluate:

- retention through the opening exchange
- whether dialogue improves understanding
- whether the distinction is immediately clear
- whether the exchanges feel natural rather than staged
- whether production complexity is justified by viewer value

No agent may abandon the Two Brents default based only on personal preference. A change requires Brent's explicit decision or evidence from produced-video review.
